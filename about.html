About Michael After Dark

Professional. Charismatic. Unforgettable. Charleston’s premier male entertainer for private events.

The Man Behind the Show

Michael After Dark began his entertainment journey in Charleston’s lively nightlife scene, where charm, rhythm, and personality matter as much as skill. Combining polished choreography, a confident stage presence, and natural Southern charisma, Michael has become one of the most recognized private entertainers along the South Carolina coast.

Drawing inspiration from icons like Magic Mike and Chippendales, Michael delivers shows that balance excitement with professionalism. Every performance is designed to celebrate the moment — whether it’s a bachelorette party, milestone birthday, or private group event — always with class, energy, and authenticity.

More Than Just a Performance

Michael believes every celebration deserves more than just entertainment — it deserves a memory that lasts. His approach focuses on connection, confidence, and respect, ensuring guests feel comfortable, energized, and completely engaged from start to finish.

From Mount Pleasant to downtown Charleston, Michael After Dark brings the energy of Sin City to South Carolina’s Lowcountry — one unforgettable night at a time.
