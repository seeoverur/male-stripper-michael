Charleston Bachelorette Party Planning Tips

  1. Book early: Weekend availability fills quickly — reserve your entertainer and venue well in advance.
  2. Check venue rules: Many Airbnb and rental properties have rules about noise and occupancy — always confirm before booking a show.
  3. Consider travel logistics: Group transportation, parking, and late-night options are common concerns in downtown Charleston.
  4. Pair with local experiences: Harbor cruises, rooftop cocktail hours, and beach mornings make the weekend feel complete.
