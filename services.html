Bachelorette Parties in Charleston

There’s a reason Charleston has become the Southeast’s top destination for bachelorette weekends — the city offers a perfect mix of southern charm, rooftop bars, and beach-house luxury. Michael After Dark brings the finishing touch to those celebrations: a professional, energetic performance designed to make the bride and her crew laugh, dance, and let go of the stress before the big day.

From Folly Beach rentals to King Street penthouses, Michael adapts to any setting with professionalism and fun. Every routine is tailored for the group — playful, confident, and full of that Magic-Mike-style energy Charleston bachelorettes love.

Birthday & Celebration Events

Mount Pleasant locals and Charleston weekenders often choose Michael After Dark to turn milestone birthdays into nights worth remembering. Whether it’s a 30th-birthday blowout, a girls’ night reunion, or a divorce-party reset, Michael brings energy that turns an ordinary evening into an unforgettable experience.

Performances are private, polished, and customized. Guests can expect charisma, charm, and an engaging show that feels more like Vegas-style entertainment than a typical club act — all brought directly to your venue in the Lowcountry.

Private House & Airbnb Shows

Some of the best parties happen away from the crowds — in private homes, rentals, or beach houses. Michael After Dark specializes in performing for small groups that want the experience of a show without the chaos of a club.

From Isle of Palms to downtown Charleston, he delivers a performance that fits the space and mood: high-energy routines, interactive fun, and the kind of professional charm that keeps every guest smiling. Each show is private, comfortable, and handled with total discretion.

Corporate & Group Entertainment

Charleston’s corporate scene might be laid-back, but private events still deserve excitement. Michael brings a tasteful, dynamic performance option for adult team outings, girls-night retreats, and post-conference parties that call for something different.

Always professional, always confident, and always fun — Michael After Dark transforms an average gathering into an experience your guests won’t stop talking about.
